State Rules

  • No attacking or scouting members of NAP alliances.
  • No recruiting, soliciting, or advertising to NAP members.
  • No attacking gatherers on resource tiles.
  • No attacking cities during fortress, facility, or stronghold battles.
  • Burning headquarters or banners requires NAP10 approval.
  • No surrounding or blocking facilities, forts or strongholds. A single touch only.
  • Each alliance is allowed one protected farm or academy.

Addendum

  • Members can't drop alliances, break rules and rejoin any NAP alliance.

Penalties to Violation of Rules
*Note: From 12-12-2024 rules.
Individuals who violate NAP rules and hold an R4 rank or below will be required to attend a meeting involving both parties' Alliance R5 representatives before any acts of force or retaliation are initiated.

Sunfire Castle Battles

Sunfire Castle Battle Protocol (as of latest agreement)

It has been agreed that the Sunfire Castle Presidency will rotate among the NAP10 alliances in accordance with the established alliance power rankings.

Castle Battle Procedure
At the beginning of each Castle Battle period, the alliance scheduled for the next presidency shall be granted 75–80 minutes to garrison the castle. Following this, 25 minutes will be allocated for other NAP10 alliances to take over the garrison.

  • Only transition rallies are permitted when handing off castle control between alliances.
  • No other rallies targeting the castle center are allowed while held by a NAP10 alliance.
  • Individual chiefs may launch solo attacks to earn battle points.
  • Alliances may alternate rallying and garrisoning the Castle Turrets.

Note: This protocol is under review following active discussions among NAP leadership regarding potential adjustments.
